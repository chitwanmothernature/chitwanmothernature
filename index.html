AT THE HEART OF CHITWAN

Discover Sauraha

Sauraha, a charming town on the edge of Chitwan National Park, is renowned for its rich biodiversity, including one-horned rhinos and Bengal tigers. Surrounded by lush forests, rivers, and Tharu villages, it’s the ideal destination for adventure, relaxation, and cultural experiences. Whether you’re exploring the jungle, cruising down the Rapti River, or experiencing local traditions, Sauraha’s serene charm provides the perfect backdrop for your stay.
